Our Process

A simple, transparent process

We keep things clear from day one. Here’s how your renovation moves from idea to handover—on time, on budget, and without surprises.

  1. 1

    Consultation

    We visit your home (or meet virtually) to understand your goals, budget, and timeline. You’ll get honest advice and an initial scope.

  2. 2

    Design & Planning

    We translate your ideas into a practical design: layouts, finishes, fixtures, and a detailed quote. Council/permit guidance included if required.

  3. 3

    Pre-Construction

    Final measurements, ordering, and scheduling. You’ll receive a clear start date and sequence so you always know what’s next.

  4. 4

    Construction

    Our licensed team completes the build with tidy, respectful workmanship. We provide progress updates and quality checks at each milestone.

  5. 5

    Handover & Aftercare

    Final walkthrough, documentation, and care tips. We stand behind our work and are here if you need anything after completion.

Note: The same proven process applies to kitchens, tailored to your scope.
